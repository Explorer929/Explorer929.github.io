Polaris Dawn

September 10, 2024, SpaceX launched the Polaris Dawn mission. This mission included the highest altitude humans have ever gone since the Apollo Program going 1,408km into orbit. The mission has also completed the first commercial spacewalk using the first SpaceX EVA spacesuits. They also performed the first laser based Starlink communication in space. On top of that, some valuable data was collected on how spacetravel will affect your health.

The mission commander's name is Jarad Isaacman. He is a pilot and a SpaceX astronaut who served as the commander for the Inspiration4 mission. Jarad Isaacman is the creator of the Polaris Program who contracted with SpaceX to create it. He has recently been chosen by Donald Trump to become the next NASA Administrator under the next administration.
