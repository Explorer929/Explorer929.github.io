All About Starlink

By Ken Kremer (CC 4 years ago)

Starlink is the world's largest satellite constellation operated by SpaceX. There are over 6,874 operational satellites in Low Earth Orbit (LEO) as of January 2025. SpaceX currently owns 1/3 of all satellites in orbit, SpaceX plans on launching a total of 42,000 Starlink satellites.

Blue Origin, a private space company owned by Jeff Bazos, plans on competing with Starlink with their Kuiper project.

T-mobile plans on partnering with Starlink to eliminate some of their deadzones.

Plans

Residential

Starlink offers $120 per month for unlimited data on the residential plan. This plan is good for users who stay at a fixed location.

Roam

This plan is good for people for people who are traveling which includes international travel. They offer 50GB of internet for $50/month or $160/month for unlimited.

Boats

This plan allows people to get access to Starlink internet while in the ocean. $250/month for 50GB and $1,000/month for 1TB of internet.
