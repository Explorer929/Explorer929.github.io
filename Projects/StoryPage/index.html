All About SpaceX

SpaceX is the world's largest private space agency. SpaceX was founded in 2002 by Elon Musk with the primary goal of landing humans on Mars

Elon Musk thinks it's important for humans to be a multiplanetary specied, but he realized that NASA had no actual plans to make that happen. As a result, he founded SpaceX in 2002 to decrease the cost of spaceflight and make humans multiplanetary.

Locations

SpaceX Headquarters

Image by SpaceX ((CC) September 28, 2012 12:29:08pm)

SpaceX Headquarters is located at Hawthorne California. They also have a series of locations they launch from.

Cape Canaveral

SpaceX launches from Cape Canaveral Space Force Station, Space Launch Complex 40. Cape Canaveral is located in Florida.

Kennedy Space Center

The Kennedy Space Center is a launch facility located at Cape Canaveral, Florida. It's run by NASA and was named after President Kennedy.

Vandenberg Space Force Base

Vandenberg Space Force Base (SFB) is located in California. It was established in 1941.

Starbase

Starbase is a launch facility located at Boca Chica, Texas. It was built in 2014 for SpaceX's Starship. It involves two launch pads and plans to build one Starship every 48 hours.
